# Chapter 10: The Void

We dropped out in deep space.

Zero climbed out of the mech. Val shoved a water bottle at him. "You're insane. But good flying."

Jax slapped his back. "Silas looked like he swallowed a lemon!"

Cas typed. "Kill-Creds are trending #1."

"Now," Zero said. "We find Pirate Haven. We fix Orion. And we burn them down."

A message from Silas: *Tuition is due. See you in the black.*

Zero deleted it.

He looked at the void.

"We are the Human Trigger," Zero whispered. "And the safety is off."
